About MnS Classes

Transforming academic lives through personalized mentoring and proven methodologies

MnS Classes – Mentoring for Success is a premier tutoring academy established in June 2019, dedicated to providing personalized coaching that transforms academic lives. We believe every student has unique potential, and our mission is to unlock it through innovative teaching, individual attention, and proven methodologies.

We specialize in Math, Science, Coding, and Language skills across multiple boards and competitive exams. Our commitment to conceptual clarity, practical problem-solving, and individual attention has resulted in measurable academic growth for 200+ students globally.

Led by an IIT Kharagpur-educated mentor, MnS Classes blends conceptual clarity with practical application using our unique TDPA methodology (Teach, Discuss, Practice, Apply). We focus on individual attention, measurable improvement, and global teaching experience.

Our Teaching Philosophy

The TDPA Methodology – A proven approach to academic excellence that ensures conceptual mastery and real-world application

📚

Teach

Clear, concept-focused instruction tailored to individual learning styles. We build strong foundations through structured, engaging lessons.

💬

Discuss

Interactive dialogue to deepen understanding and resolve doubts. Every question is welcomed, every concept is explored thoroughly.

✏️

Practice

Rigorous problem-solving with guided practice and immediate feedback. Mastery comes through consistent, focused practice.

🚀

Apply

Real-world application and mastery assessment for lasting learning. Connect concepts to practical scenarios and exam success.

Why TDPA Works

  • Personalized Learning Plans: Unique to each student's pace, learning style, and academic goals
  • Scholastic Improvement: Supports students to improve their scholastics through structured methodology
  • Optimized Time: Every second spent together is optimized for success and maximum learning impact
  • Conceptual Mastery: Focus on conceptual clarity, not rote learning – building long-term academic foundations
  • Measurable Growth: Track progress with regular assessments and personalized feedback
  • Confidence Building: Builds confidence and intellectual curiosity in learners
